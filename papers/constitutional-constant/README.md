## Summary

This paper maps the formal algebra of collapse-based computation to special relativity and nuclear physics. Three results are presented.

1. **c = G₀** — The speed of light satisfies every formal property of the algebra's constitutional constraint: it defines the structure of the space (the Minkowski metric), is invariant across all reference frames, cannot be exceeded from within the system, and its violation produces logical contradiction.
2. **E₀ = mc² = ω₀** — Einstein's mass-energy equivalence is the formal statement that the fixed point is a function of the constitution: ω₀ = m · G₀².
3. **Iron-56 = ω₀ of nuclear physics** — The unique maximum of the binding energy per nucleon curve. The nuclear fixed point enables the biological fixed point through PHD enzyme cofactors, linking nuclear physics to HIF-1α regulation through a single element.
