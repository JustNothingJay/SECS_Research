# Existence as Fixed Point: A Synthesis of Deterministic Collapse Theory (v2)

**Author:** Jay Andrew Carpenter
**Date:** March 2026

## Summary

This paper presents a unified account of existence grounded in a single algebraic structure: the collapse operator C, whose image is {ω₀, ∅}. Four companion papers establish the formal algebra, prove its applicability to all natural systems via the exhaustive veto partition of death, connect it to seven open mathematical frontiers through the distinction between terminal and generative fixed points, and advance the existential claim that existence itself IS the fixed point.

This is the second-generation synthesis, integrating results from the constitutional constant mapping and the gestational biology programme into the meta-theoretic framework.
