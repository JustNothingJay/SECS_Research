# Contributing to SECS Research

This repo is a **publication mirror**. Paper content is generated by SECS_Sovereign tooling and published here via a controlled transfer script.

## Rules

- **PRs that modify paper content will not be merged.** All paper content originates in the source repo (SECS_Sovereign) and is published here in a controlled, hash-verified process.
- **If you find errors in a paper**, open an issue describing the error. Corrections will be made in the source repo and republished.
- **PRs for repo infrastructure** (README formatting, CI, tooling) are welcome.
