## 3.2. Death Satisfies All Three

For any natural system $\mathcal{N}$ with finite energy and positive entropy production:

- **Binary:** Every state is viable or non-viable. No third category.
- **Exhaustive:** Death applies to every natural system without exception. The second law of thermodynamics guarantees this. - Viability function: $\nu : \mathcal{P}_{\mathcal{N}} \to \{0, 1\}$

**Theorem.** Let $\mathcal{N}$ be any natural system with finite energy and positive entropy production. Then $\mathcal{N}$ admits an exhaustive veto partition, and the law of the excluded middle holds over $\mathcal{P}_{\mathcal{N}}$:
