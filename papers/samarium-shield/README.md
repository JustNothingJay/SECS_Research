## Summary

The Klein bottle is topologically two Möbius strips glued at their boundary, and its diagonal eigenvalue scaled by √2 yields promethium (Z=61). Both technetium and promethium decay exclusively through weak-force channels — the nuclear-scale repair station.

**Keywords:** samarium, promethium, Klein bottle, nuclear remediation, weak force, constraint surface
