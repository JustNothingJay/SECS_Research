# The Story: How a Substrate Became a Theory of Everything

**Author:** Jay Andrew Carpenter
**Date:** March 2026

## Summary

This is not a paper. It is the story of how a paper — several papers — came to exist. It is written for anyone who encounters the formal work and asks: *where did this come from?*

A narrative account of the research programme's origins and development, from the SECS computational substrate through biological research to the formal algebra and its physical mappings.
