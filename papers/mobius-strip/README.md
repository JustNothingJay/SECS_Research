# The Möbius Strip as Fixed Point of Existence: Cradle to the Grave

[![DOI](https://zenodo.org/badge/DOI/10.5281/zenodo.19020526.svg)](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.19020526)

**Author:** Jay Andrew Carpenter  
**Published:** [Zenodo](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.19020526) (DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.19020526)

## Summary

The constraint surface on which oxygen fixed points operate has Möbius strip topology — a one-sided, non-orientable surface formed with a half twist. This resolves the simultaneity of forward and reverse processes, velocity fields of elemental fixed points, and the nature of friction and Le Chatelier feedback.

**Keywords:** Möbius strip, fixed point, oxygen, constraint surface, Le Chatelier, topology
