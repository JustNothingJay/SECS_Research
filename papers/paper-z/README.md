## Summary

One equation with no measured inputs produces α⁻¹ = 137.035999177 and its polynomial right-hand side generates successive eigenvalues that when floored yield atomic numbers 137, 43, 13, 4, 1 — each mapping to a fundamental force and naming specific elements, with water structure (two hydrogens, one oxygen) encoded geometrically.

**Keywords:** periodic table, fine structure constant, eigenvalue tower, fundamental forces, water geometry
